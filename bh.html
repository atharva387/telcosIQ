Bharatbhushan Kolhe

About Me

Mr. Kolhe is a strong believer in the popular ethic, "Tomorrow never waits," so whatever is there, you need to do it today. An Engineering Graduate of 1997, Mr. Kolhe is a veteran authority in setting up distribution networks. He has been instrumental in the industry for more than two decades.

Prior to his current role in Priya Group, he held key positions with renowned brands like Amul, Hutchison Essar, Reliance Communications, Virgin Mobiles, and Jio. He drives business with systems and processes, building profitable work relationships. He is known for his futuristic vision in channelizing & distributing.
