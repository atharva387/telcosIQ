About Me

Having graduated in Commerce and completed her MBA in Finance, Jyoti joined the group in its early days as a trainee accountant. With her enthusiasm, thirst for knowledge, and drive, she has donned many caps, from starting as a trainee to now heading our latest venture, E Shop.

Her attention to detail, involvement in every aspect of the firm, and passion for the business make her a great leader. She has proved her expertise in various domains like financial controllership, banking, accounting, planning, analysis, and audit. Her relentless journey of the last 15 years has made her an asset to our organization.
