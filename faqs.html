Frequently Asked Questions

What is a short code?

A short code is a 5-6 digit number used for sending and receiving SMS and MMS messages.

How can I lease a short code?

You can lease a short code through a service provider or directly with a carrier.

Difference between shared and dedicated short codes?

Shared short codes are used by multiple businesses, while dedicated short codes are exclusively yours.

What is the cost of getting a short code?

The cost varies depending on the type (dedicated or shared) and usage.

Industries that benefit the most?

Retail, healthcare, education, and marketing industries commonly use short codes for customer engagement.

How long does it take to activate a short code?

Short code activation can take anywhere from 4-8 weeks depending on the carrier approval process.

Is there support for global short codes?

Yes, some service providers offer global short codes, but availability varies by country.

What are the compliance requirements for using a short code?

Short code users must comply with carrier regulations, including opt-in and opt-out mechanisms for end users.

Can I transfer my short code to another provider?

Yes, it is possible to transfer a short code, but it requires coordination between carriers and providers.
