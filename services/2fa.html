Overview

Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) adds an additional layer of security to your online accounts by requiring a second form of verification, such as a one-time password sent via SMS.

Key Features

  • Secure OTP generation for user verification.
  • Integration with various platforms and applications.
  • Real-time delivery of authentication messages.
