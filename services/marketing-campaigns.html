Overview

Marketing campaigns using SMS allow businesses to reach customers directly with promotional offers, updates, and important information, maximizing engagement and conversion rates.

Key Features

  • Segmented lists for targeted messaging.
  • Automated campaigns for seamless execution.
  • Real-time analytics and reporting.
