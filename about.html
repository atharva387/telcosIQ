About Us

Founded by Mr. Bharatbhushan Kolhe and Ms.Jyoti Bajpai, our company has positioned itself as a pioneering leader in the field of short code services. With a passion for enhancing customer engagement, we specialize in providing dedicated and shared short code messaging solutions that empower businesses to connect with their audiences more effectively. Since our inception, we have been committed to driving innovation in communication technology, adapting to the evolving landscape of digital marketing

Our Vision

Our mission is to empower businesses with reliable, scalable, and innovative short code solutions. We strive to enhance customer communication, enabling companies of all sizes to engage their clients through effective SMS and MMS messaging campaigns. By leveraging our expertise, we aim to transform the way businesses interact with their customers, fostering loyalty and driving growth.
